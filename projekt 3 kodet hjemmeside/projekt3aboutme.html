About me

My name is Luka and I use the pronounce they/them. I'm 30 years old, or 29+ as I prefer to say, and I live in a cozy little appartment in Odense with my two lovely bunnies Kaisen & Karlo

I was born and raised in Denmark, but lived two years in Spain with my parents when I was a kid.

Right now I'm studying Multimedia Design in Odense, and in my sparetime I'm an Amazon Influencer, Instagrammer, and a writer

I've always been a creative person, especially when it comes to writing stories. I'm currently the author of two web-series which can be read onTapas, and I'm already working on a third.

I also very much enjoy reading, and my instagram is low-key famous for my very beautiful and colorful bookshelves.

I have worked with different authors and publishers for a couple of years now, where they sent me an advanced readers copy of a book in exhange for an honest review or for editing.

My dream, and goal with this education is to be able to create book covers, websites etc.
